Helping to facilitate right care at the right time

During emergency situations, it is now quite common that patients who are rushed to nearby hospitals often face great hardships due to non-availability of the required facilities in that hospital. At the hospital, staffs are overworked; managing huge incumbent crowds at the reception and unable to prioritize emergency cases and critical situations. It would be extremely helpful if the patient knows of hospitals closest to their location and before reaching is aware whether the necessary medical facilities are available for providing the right care. For hospitals, a heads-up would ensure well prepared staff and onsite facilities to provide timely care.

Capgemini India's aarogya niketan platform has been developed precisely with the objective of facilitating discovery of nearest hospitals and medical care for patients and delivery of timely care by hospitals, especially to emergency COVID cases. Think about Zomato when you want to order food from a nearby restaurant or book the nearest Ola when you want to travel urgently. The same principle should apply with even much higher importance when you want to book a medical service, especially during an emergency.
